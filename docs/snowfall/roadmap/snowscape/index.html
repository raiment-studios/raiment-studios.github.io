Snowscape

The Snowscape engine (which this roadmap is primarily focused on at the moment) is a custom voxel rendering engine motivated by learning WGPU and Rust. Intended to be a voxel engine specifically for the requirements of Snowfall, not a general game engine (like Bevy, for example).

v0.0 (wip)​

While scope creep is a concern, v0.0 is more of an exploration of getting a basic engine going, rather than a specific MVP target.
